Our Philosophy

Honest Food,
Honest Fire

At Ember & Oak we believe the best flavors come from the simplest techniques. Our open kitchen burns oak and applewood every night, kissing each plate with something you can't replicate on a gas burner.

Our chefs source produce weekly from Hudson Valley farms, and our menu shifts with the season—because the best ingredient is always whatever is freshest today.
